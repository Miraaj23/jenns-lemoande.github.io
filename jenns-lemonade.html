Jenn's Story

Jenn began her lemonade empire one hot and sunny day in July of 1999, with her only product being fresh lemonade.

"I really wanted a doll that I had seen at a local toy store, but my parents didn't have the most money, so I decided to earn money save up for it."

Inspired by her grandmother's homemade lemonade that she made every 4th of July, Jenn decided to sell lemonade in order to save up for that doll.

"I didn't even know how to make lemonade, so I had to call my grandmother and ask her how she does it. It was more work than I thought it would be!"

After a couple hours,Jenn was able to perfect her grandmother's lemonade & she set up a chair and table with a sign taped to the front advertising her lemonade for just a dollar a cup.

I didn't really make much the first day, even week, but I made at least 3-5 dollars a day & that was enough to keep me going.
